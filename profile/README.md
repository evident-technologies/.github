# Evident Technologies

Building court-grade software that makes truth legible, durable, and affordable.

Evident Technologies is a legal-technology organization focused on evidence integrity, due process, and accountable systems design.  
Our mission is to democratize access to justice by delivering professional eDiscovery and evidence workflows to teams priced out of legacy platforms.

## What We Build

- **Evident ICU**: court-grade eDiscovery and evidence processing
- **Deterministic forensic pipeline tooling**
- **Shared engineering libraries** for design, services, and compliance
- **Practical legal-tech products** for solo practitioners, small firms, and mission-aligned teams

## Operating Principles

- Truth before persuasion
- Integrity before convenience
- Due process before outcomes
- Auditability by default
- Long-term defensibility over short-term trend chasing

## Engineering Commitments

- Original evidence remains immutable
- SHA-256 hashing for evidence and derivatives
- Append-only audit and custody records
- Deterministic and reproducible processing
- Accessibility and security as first-class requirements
