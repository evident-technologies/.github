# Code of Conduct — Evident Technologies LLC

**Effective Date:** 2026-05-22
**Governing Entity:** Evident Technologies LLC, a New Jersey Limited Liability Company

---

## Purpose

Evident Technologies LLC builds legal-technology infrastructure for attorneys, public defenders, and litigants navigating the justice system. This work demands the highest standards of professional conduct from every person who interacts with this organization — whether as an authorized contributor, a licensed client, or a member of the public engaging through GitHub.

This Code of Conduct defines the behavioral expectations that protect the integrity of the platform, the dignity of the individuals it serves, and the credibility of Evident Technologies LLC as a legal-technology institution.

---

## Who This Applies To

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All authorized contributors and contractors
- All employees and managers of Evident Technologies LLC
- All licensed clients interacting via GitHub issues, discussions, or support channels
- Any member of the public engaging with repositories, issues, or public-facing content in this organization

---

## Expected Standards

### Professional Conduct

All participants are expected to:

- Communicate with factual accuracy and professional restraint
- Treat all parties with respect regardless of personal, legal, or adversarial differences
- Represent their identity, credentials, and affiliation honestly
- Maintain confidentiality of any non-public information encountered in this context

### Platform Integrity

Given that Evident Technologies software may be used in active legal proceedings:

- Do not misrepresent the capabilities, limitations, or outputs of the platform
- Do not attempt to demonstrate vulnerabilities, manipulations, or bypasses outside of the coordinated disclosure process described in [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md)
- Do not introduce, reference, or upload real case data, PII, or legally privileged materials into any public channel

### Prohibited Conduct

The following are prohibited without exception:

- Harassment, intimidation, or targeted personal attacks of any kind
- Discriminatory language or conduct based on race, ethnicity, national origin, sex, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, religion, or any protected characteristic
- Deliberate misrepresentation of identity or credentials
- Unauthorized access to, or disclosure of, confidential or proprietary information
- Any conduct that would constitute a violation of New Jersey or federal law
- Attempts to weaponize this platform, its issue tracker, or its communications infrastructure for litigation strategy without proper legal process

---

## Enforcement

### Reporting

Violations of this Code of Conduct may be reported to:

**Email:** `legal@evidtech.com`
**Subject:** `[CONDUCT] <brief description>`

Reports are reviewed by the Manager of Evident Technologies LLC. Anonymous reports are accepted but may limit our ability to investigate or respond.

### Consequences

Evident Technologies LLC reserves the right, at its sole discretion, to:

- Remove any content that violates this Code of Conduct
- Close issues, pull requests, or discussions without response
- Revoke access to any platform surface or communication channel
- Terminate contributor or client agreements
- Pursue legal remedies where conduct constitutes a violation of law or a breach of contract

### No Arbitration of Conduct Disputes

Conduct enforcement decisions by Evident Technologies LLC are not subject to appeal, arbitration, or external review. The Manager's determination is final.

---

## Scope of This Document

This Code of Conduct governs behavior in GitHub-hosted spaces operated by Evident Technologies LLC. It does not govern the conduct of clients, attorneys, or parties in active legal proceedings, which are subject to applicable rules of professional conduct, court rules, and governing law.
